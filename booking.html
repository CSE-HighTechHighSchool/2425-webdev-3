Location

The new ALTO Museum, a joint effort by Aqua Lifeline and Thrive Ocean, is now open in Wagga Wagga, Australia.


Address: New South Wales 2650, Australia


The museum is open every day of the week, running from 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM on the weekdays and 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM on the weekends.

Seasonal Specials

Session 1: Winter Wonder Whirl


Date: January 12, 2025

Time: 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM


This winter-themed session invites visitors to discover the ways marine life adapts to seasonal changes and to witness live animal feedings. Learn about the critical threats these creatures face through engaging talks and discussions led by conservationists. The tour includes a special 3D dome showing of “Waves of Change,” a documentary illustrating pollution’s impact on Australian waters. Guests will finish with a curated digital tour along parts of the eastern coast, spotlighting native wildlife and ongoing conservation programs.

Session 2: Spring Seas Special


Date: April 15, 2025

Time: 9:30 AM – 1:30 PM


Celebrate spring with an in-depth journey through ALTO Museum’s exhibits, featuring marine animal feedings and a thought-provoking talk on pollution’s effects on aquatic species. The highlight of this tour is a 3D dome screening of “Ocean Reefs: A Fragile Paradise,” a documentary that immerses visitors in the dazzling colors and life of Australia’s coral reefs. End the day with an outdoor virtual coastal tour, tracing the wonders of Australia's southwestern shoreline, focusing on both natural beauty and the pressing need for environmental conservation.

Session 3: Summer Splash Special


Date: July 20, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM


Dive into an interactive exploration at the ALTO Museum, where you'll witness live animal feedings and engage in hands-on exhibits that highlight the effects of pollution and global warming on Australia’s marine life. After the museum tour, step inside the immersive 3D dome to experience “Life Below the Surface,” a documentary showcasing the vibrant, delicate underwater ecosystems along Australia’s coastlines. Perfect for families and marine enthusiasts alike, this summer tour gives a deep look at how we can protect and preserve these incredible environments.

Session 4: Autumn Aquatic Adventure


Date: October 18, 2025

Time: 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM

The Autumn Aquatic Adventure offers a guided museum experience featuring live animal feedings and in-depth sessions on the impact of global warming. In the immersive 3D dome, the “Guardians of the Coastline” documentary brings Australia's marine ecosystems to life. The day concludes with a virtual exploration of the Great Ocean Road, highlighting coastal conservation efforts along one of Australia’s most iconic shorelines.
