Organizations

Aqua Lifeline

Our organization provides ocean exhibition/tours to let people learn more about the ocean and spread awareness about the problems. Additionally, we provide information about sea life and why we should focus on the problems regarding the ocean.

Thrive Ocean

Our organization aims to resolve problems regarding the ocean and research further solutions. We have a major focus on identifying issues regarding sea life, rising sea levels, and ocean pollution.

Problems

Pollution

Marine pollution occurs when human-made substances, like waste from factories, farms, and homes, along with noise and invasive species, enter the ocean and cause harm.

Warming

Ocean warming is a significant impact of climate change, resulting from the ocean absorbing extra heat and energy from increasing greenhouse gas emissions.

Endangerment

Marine animal endangerment occurs when species face threats like overfishing and pollution, leading to declining populations and increased risk of extinction.

Destruction

Habitat destruction refers to the damage or loss of underwater ecosystems, such as coral reefs and mangroves, often caused by threats like pollution and coastal development.
